Pixel Park operates under a dual-entity structure designed to bridge international finance with Pakistani sovereign territory.

## 7.1 Corporate Entities
* **Pixel Park SMC Pvt. Ltd. (The Operator):** Registered with the **SECP** and licensed by the **STZA**. This entity holds the STZ license and manages the "Digital Court" of the city.
* **Dagmar Pvt. Ltd. (The Asset Co):** The physical owner of the Gatwala Commercial Hub. It issues the $SIKKA inventory and manages the Liquidity Treasury.

## 7.2 The "Zombie Protocol" (KYC Compliance)
To prevent money laundering and satisfy FBR/STZA requirements:
*   All assets (Nomads/Desks) are **White-listed**. 
*   If an asset is transferred to a non-KYC wallet, it enters a **"Zombie State"**. 
*   The asset remains locked until the new owner completes verification on the Pixel Park Dashboard.

## 7.3 Tax Residency
Citizens holding a **Pixel Desk** are legally entitled to a **Zone Enterprise License**, granting a 10-year holiday on all income and turnover taxes, as per the STZA Act 2020.
